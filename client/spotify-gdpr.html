How it works

The GDPR is a European law that requires tech companies to let you download the data that they have collected about you. It turns out that Spotify has a record of every time that you stream any song, and that information can be turned into colourful graphs and charts!

This service will take your GDPR data and give you a detailed look at your own listening history and how your listening patterns have changed over time.

Privacy

Unlike with our music analysis service, we do not store any information about you when you submit your GDPR data. All of the analysis is performed in your browser, and the only requests sent to the server are to match song names to artists, genres, and albums.

You will need to log into Spotify with your account because we can't get that information from them unless you log in. The login prompt will say that we are requesting access to your subscription details. We need this permission in order to search for songs.
